Discover University of Minnesota Duluth (UMD)

Learn why I chose UMD, what I love about Duluth and the surrounding area, and some personal campus highlights.

Why I chose University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose UMD because of its strong programs in business,, the smaller campus community that supports meaningful faculty connections, and opportunities for hands-on projects and internships. The balance between strong academics and a welcoming campus culture made it the right fit for me.

  • Smaller class sizes and accessible faculty
  • Strong experiential learning and internship pipeline
  • Close-knit student community and active student organizations

What I like about Duluth & the surrounding area

Duluth sits on the shores of Lake Superior — the outdoor opportunities are incredible. I love that I can go hiking, kayaking, or just walk along the lake after classes. The city has a lively arts scene, local cafes, and community events that make it feel like home.

  • Lake Superior shoreline and lakeside trails
  • Local shops, restaurants, and community events
  • Year-round outdoor recreation (hiking, skiing, kayaking)

Personal Experiences & Campus Highlights

During my time at UMD I've participated in student organizations, worked on team projects, and taken advantage of experiences that expanded my skills. A few highlights:

  • Worked on a semester-long marketing project for Minnesuing Acres.
  • Volunteered at Engager Tower.
  • Participated in a case competition for a local business.

These experiences gave me practical skills and memorable connections — both academically and socially — that shaped my college experience.
