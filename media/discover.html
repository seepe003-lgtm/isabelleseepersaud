Discover UMD

Explore the University of Minnesota Duluth and the beautiful city of Duluth through my perspective as a student.

University of Minnesota Duluth

UMD offers a world-class education with a close-knit campus community. The Labovitz School of Business & Economics provides excellent opportunities for marketing students with hands-on learning experiences, internship programs, and connections to regional businesses.

The campus combines modern facilities with beautiful natural surroundings, creating an inspiring environment for learning and personal growth.

City of Duluth

Duluth is a vibrant city situated on the shores of Lake Superior. Known for its stunning natural beauty, outdoor recreation opportunities, and rich cultural scene, it's the perfect place to balance academic life with adventure.

From hiking trails to local businesses, Duluth offers countless opportunities to explore, learn, and grow both personally and professionally.
