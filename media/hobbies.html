My Hobbies & Activities

Here are some of the activities and interests that shape who I am outside of academics and work.

Fostering Rescue Dogs

I volunteer with Wags & Whiskers Animal Rescue of Minnesota, fostering dogs in need of temporary homes. This experience has taught me responsibility, compassion, and the importance of giving back to the community. I've helped socialize and care for dogs preparing for adoption, contributing over 100 volunteer hours to animal welfare.

American Marketing Association

As an active member of the American Marketing Association at UMD, I participate in professional development workshops, networking events, and case study competitions. This involvement helps me stay current with marketing trends and build connections within the industry.

Photography

Photography allows me to capture and share the beauty I see in everyday moments. Whether it's campus life, nature, or events, I enjoy using visual storytelling to document experiences and express creativity.

Women in Business

I participate in Women in Business initiatives that focus on empowering women in professional environments. Through workshops, networking events, and mentorship opportunities, I develop leadership skills and connect with other driven women in business.
